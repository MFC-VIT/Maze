Maze Game

    Leaderboard



      Instructions

      Movement Controls:

      Use the arrow keys to navigate your character.

      Objective:

      Avoid being caught by the Chaser.

      Collect coins to increase your score. Each coin is worth 10 points.

      Scoring:

      Accumulate points by collecting coins throughout the levels.

      Starting the Game:

      Select your desired difficulty level to begin.

      For maximum points, it is recommended to start from the easy level and progress through to the final level.

      Level Goals:

      In each level, your primary objective is to obtain the jewel.

      Successfully collecting the jewel before being caught by the Chaser will allow you to advance to the next level.
